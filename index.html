How to Play:

Welcome to Neon Defense!

Neon City is under attack. We need you to defend it! To accomplish this task, you'll find several turrets with differents properties such as :

  • AutoTurret

    This turret is the basic turret.But it does the job!

  • LaserTurret

    This turret shoots a laser and deals damage on time.

  • ShotGunTurret

    This turret shoots several bullets on a short range! Ideal for a burst !

  • UserControlledTurret

    This turret allows you to aim at your enemies ! It's the weakness of drones

And you can use different Power Turrets such as the Fire, the Electric, and the Freeze one

You'll be meeting a lot of differents enemies while defending Neon City

Make sure to use all the abilities you have to survive!

To place a turret, select the turret you want to place, and click on the blue circle on the map.

Power turrets have predefined emplacements and a global range.

Drones are only affected by usercontrolled turrets.

Everytime an enemy manages to get through the Neon Sun , you lose a life.

If you run out of hearts, Neon City is lost and taken over by the Robot Army.
