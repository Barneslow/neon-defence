How to Play:


Welcome to Neon Defense!

Neon City is under attack. We need you to defend it!


  1. Use the in-game resources to construct and place turrets strategically.
  2. To place a turret, click on the type of turrets you'd like to place.
  3. Then click on the tile with the little sun to place it.
  4. Repeat until finished.
  5. You can sell a turret by clicking on it , but it'll give you half the resources it cost back.
  6. Press the Start Button. You can also pause the Game anytime, by pressing the pause button.
  7. During the Game you can use the Power Turrets. First you need to purchase these. Each has their own unique ability (Fire, Electrify and Freeze). Power Turrets are automatically placed when you click on their buttons and are not moveable.

You'll be meeting a lot of different enemies while defending Neon City. Make sure to use all the abilities you have to survive!

Drones are only affected by user-controlled turrets.

Every time an enemy manages to get through the Neon Sun, you lose a life. If you run out of hearts, Neon City is lost and taken over by the Robot Army.



Game Mechanics explained:

To accomplish this task, you'll find several turrets with different properties such as:

  • Auto Turret:

    This turret is the basic turret. But it does the job!

  • Laser Turret:

    This turret shoots a laser and deals damage over time.

  • Shotgun Turret:

    This turret shoots several bullets at a short range! Ideal for bursts!

  • Human controlled Turret:

    This turret is controlled by you. It allows you, the player, to take control and manually aim and shoot at enemies.

You can also use different Power Turrets such as:

  • Fire:

    This turret burns the enemies.

  • Electric:

    This turret electrifies the enemies.

  • Freeze:

    This turret freezes the enemies.
